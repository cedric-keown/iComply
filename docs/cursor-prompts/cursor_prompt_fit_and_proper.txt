CURSOR PROMPT: FIT & PROPER MANAGEMENT MODULE
==============================================

Create a fully functional, realistic HTML mockup for the Fit & Proper Management module of a South African FAIS broker compliance portal. This module tracks qualifications, regulatory exams (RE1/RE5), Class of Business (COB) training, experience, and good standing requirements.

## BRAND & DESIGN REQUIREMENTS

**Brand Colors:**
- Primary (Teal/Turquoise): #5CBDB4
- Text (Charcoal Grey): #4A4A4A
- Background: #FFFFFF
- Light Grey: #F8F9FA
- Success: #28A745
- Warning: #FFC107
- Danger: #DC3545
- Info: #17A2B8

**Framework & Theme:**
- Use Bootstrap 5+ with Phoenix v1.23.0 theme: https://prium.github.io/phoenix/v1.23.0/index.html
- Fully responsive design (mobile-first)
- Vanilla JavaScript only (no frameworks)
- South African locale (ZAR currency, DD/MM/YYYY dates)

## MODULE STRUCTURE

### Page Layout: Tabbed Interface with Side Navigation

**Left Sidebar (Fixed on desktop, hamburger on mobile):**
- Overview Dashboard (icon: grid)
- Qualifications & Credentials (icon: graduation cap)
- Regulatory Examinations (icon: file-text)
- Class of Business Training (icon: book)
- Experience & Employment (icon: briefcase)
- Good Standing (icon: shield-check)

**Main Content Area:**
Each section has its own view with consistent header pattern:
- Section title
- Status badge (Compliant/Non-compliant/Pending)
- Last updated date
- Quick action buttons
