Key Risks & Mitigation

Risk 1: Kotive Market Dominance

Established market presence with proven product and FSCA integration

Mitigation:
  • Emphasize brokerage-specific features
  • Target smaller FSPs (Kotive too expensive)
  • Superior representative experience
  • Faster, more focused support

Risk 2: Service Provider Bundling

CTB/Masthead could bundle software more aggressively to retain clients

Mitigation:
  • "Take control" alternative positioning
  • Demonstrate cost savings
  • Target FSPs wanting internal management
  • Migration assistance from service providers

Risk 3: International Platform Localization

VComply, SimpleRisk, or Quantivate could partner with SA compliance firm

Mitigation:
  • Maintain SA-specific feature velocity
  • Deep FSCA integration (hard to replicate)
  • Local customer support
  • SA regulatory change responsiveness
